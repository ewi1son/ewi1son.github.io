Front-End Week 2

The development process, for as complex as it is, is relatively straightforward. Pseudocoding is incredibly important and just as easy to overlook. Spending time pseudocoding assures that you have a solid grasp on concepts.

To develop good code is to be efficient, and to remain as consistent as possible. Being a develper is almost one in the same, while also being good teammate. Programmers have, and always will, hate to click things they know don’t work. And also resizing.

Overall, this has been a week that I’ve really enjoyed. Learning how to be a developer has not been easy, and is definitely one of the more challenging things that I have done. Wrapping my head around these topics at such a fast pace has been tough, but it has been a fun journey so far.
